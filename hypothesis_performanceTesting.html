What We Analyze

Athleticism Distilled for Soccer

We don't measure generic fitness. Every metric is tied to on-field performance — how you move, how efficiently you generate power, and where technique is costing you.

Jump

Explosive Power, Optimized

Not just "jump higher." We analyze loading mechanics, takeoff angles, and landing patterns to build power that translates to winning headers, shielding the ball, and changing direction.

Sprint

Speed Built for the Pitch

Not just "run faster." We break down stride mechanics, ground contact time, and hip drive to make you faster in the ways that win 50/50 balls and create separation from defenders.

Kick

Strike with Precision & Power

We quantify plant-foot positioning, hip rotation, and follow-through mechanics. The result: harder, more accurate shots and passes — with less injury risk from poor form.
